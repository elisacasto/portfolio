NICE TO MEET YOU
My name is Elisa Casto. With over 6 years of experience in UX/UI Design, I am dedicated to improving digital products to enhance users' lives. I work closely with developers, product managers and other stakeholders to design experiences that balance functionality and aesthetics. My approach is based on empathy, accountability and effective communication, key elements to create innovative solutions and exceptional user experiences.

I graduated in 2018 in Web Design at Bau University, in Barcelona, where I was able to enrich my knowledge in subjects such as UX and UI Design. I'm very curious and I'm eager to learn as much as I can. Learn from mistakes and move forward to improve. Being flexible in changes and yes, I love being adventurous! My goal is to achieve my dreams, as I like to fight for what I really want.
