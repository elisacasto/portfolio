"Il mio sogno in un book"

University project of Editorial Design

In my last year of university, I developed an illustrated sketchbook about my erasmus adventure. The objective of this project was to find a balance between text and image. I have designed the illustrations with Adobe Photoshop. And edited the pages with Adobe InDesign.
