Home for Swap

Category: UI/UX

Client: Collaborative Perks

Year: 2019

Home for Swap is a platform that promotes home exchange among people from all over the world, in order to make known a different way of travelling, away from mass tourism, responsible with the environment and that promotes the mutual enrichment of the participants.

The objective

One of the objectives, beyond the restyling of the platform, was to reorganize the categories and functionalities, optimizing the user experience. The first step, was to establish priorities and our target, emphasize with the user to understand him needs.
