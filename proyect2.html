The project

Home for Swap is a platform that promotes home exchange among people from all over the world, in order to let a diferent way a travelling be known, away from mass tourism, responsible with the environment and that promotes the mutual enrichment of the participants.

A better experience

One of the objectives, beyond the restyling of the platform, was to reorganize the categories and functionalities, optimizing the user experience. The first step, was to establish priorities and our target, to emphasize to the users and to understand their needs. We are focused on a linear design, easy and accessible to all. We reorganized the architecture of the content by dividing it into categories and optimizing the navigation menu. We designed an interface that improves the user experience and found a solution for all devices.
