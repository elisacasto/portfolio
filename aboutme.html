Nice to meet you

My name is Elisa Casto. I am a webs designer, who graduated in 2016 in Editorial Design at the Academy of Fine Arts in Lecce, Italy.

I am very curious and eager to learn as much as I can. Learning from mistakes and moving forward to improve myself.

People consider me a very brave girl. Being flexible in changes and yes, I love being adventurous!

My objective is to reach my dreams, as I like to fight for what I really want. Thanks to my Erasmus experience in Madrid I was able to grow as a person and professionally.

I have lived one year in Barcelona, ​​where I studied a master's degree in web design. Where I was able to enrich my knowledge in subjects such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, User experience and User interface.
